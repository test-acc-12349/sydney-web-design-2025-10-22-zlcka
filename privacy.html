4. Cookie Policy

4.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that contain information about your browsing activity. They help us remember your preferences, improve your user experience, and track website analytics. Cookies may be either "persistent" (remaining on your device until you delete them) or "session-based" (deleted when you close your browser).

4.2 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies:

    Required for website functionality. These cannot be disabled without affecting site performance.

  • Performance Cookies:

    Help us understand how visitors use our website, including pages visited and errors encountered.

  • Functional Cookies:

    Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your browsing experience.

  • Marketing Cookies:

    Used to deliver targeted advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

4.3 Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies, or receive a notification when a cookie is being set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and appearance of our website.

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org or your browser's help documentation.
